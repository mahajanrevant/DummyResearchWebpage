RobotKnights

Apeejay School Pitampura

RobotKnights participated in the inaugral Delhi Robotics League 2023. Each match of the competition was split into two parts:

  • Striker Mode: Displace “pot balls” by launching "shot balls" and then push "pot balls" into "pot holes" for extra points
  • Blocker Mode: The robot would use cylinderical discs to block "pot holes" to limit scoring oppurtunities for shooting team.

My Contributions

I was the team leader for the team and the sub lead for mechanical design, part selection, and build process of the robot.

The design process involved leading brainstorming session with the team to come up with ideas, sketching designs and creating CAD models to visualize them, and quickly prototyping them to validate them. The prototyping gave us valuable results that were used for iterating on the design.

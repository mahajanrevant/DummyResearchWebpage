Mechanical Mechanisms

This page contains the various mechanisms I read about in textbooks and research papers and my attempt at re-creating them.


Integrated Movement

Using an angular gripper attached to a 3 wheeled robot to pick up and place objects.

Using dual IR sensors make a 3 wheeled robot to follow a black line across steep bends.


Scissor Lift

Simple scissor lift mechanism with a step up gear ratio for higher torque

Fork Lift

Using rack and pinion mechanism to create a forklift

Crane

Using a pulley system to create a crane with a hook


Ball Shooter

Using rubber band to coil a shooting plate. When released, shoots the ball

Ball Shooter

Turning gears to release the rubber band which directly shoots the ball

Ball Shooter

Rotating a lever with gears to hit the ball at the end of rotation


Home Automations

Prototype to turn on/off fan based on if someone leaves the room (Please Turn on Audio)

Prototype to open and close dustbin when someone waives hand close to it.


Steering Mechanisms

Using a rack and pinion to turn the front axel on a 4-wheeled robot.

Using spherical gear to turn the front castor wheel on a 3-wheeled robot.


Stamping Mechanisms

Using a rack and pinion to repeatedly stamp.

Using a rack and pinion to stamp and move the paper.
